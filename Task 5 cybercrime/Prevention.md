#PART B – Prevention Checklist
##Stay Safe Online – Checklist

✔️ General Safety Tips

•	Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone

•	Do not click on unknown or suspicious links 

•	Verify calls claiming to be from banks or officials 

•	Use strong and unique passwords for accounts

•	Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible 

•	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions 


💳 Financial / UPI Safety

•	Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money 

•	Do not scan QR codes from unknown sources


🚨 Report Cybercrime
•	Report at: National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) 

•	Helpline: 1930
